Frequently Asked
Questions

What does the car rental actually cover?

The rental includes delivery or collection of the hire vehicle, plus a daily mileage quota. This varies from provider to provider. Supercars are often limited to 250 kilometers a day. Economy cars, SUVs, and similar often have a limit of around 250 to 300 kilometers per day. Exceeding the mileage limit will result in an additional fee. Additionally, customers receive car insurance, civil liability insurance, and more.

Do you have any restrictions or policies for off-road driving?

Although some customers request this on account of our range of SUVs, we are unable to authorize off-road driving. Our rental service does not extend to off-roading. However, this is possible by contacting specialist companies in the Europe.

Do you provide a car
delivery service?

Delivery be arranged on a case by case basis. In most situations, delivery of a car to a prearranged destination is possible. Most of our partners provide this service. But some may limit delivery to particular nearby locations.

At what point will my deposit be refunded?

The deposit will be refunded when the rental agreement has been completed. After the car is handed back in good condition and fully inspected, the deposit will be released. However, it can take up to 28 days for the amount to fully process. This can sometimes be the case in the event of extra costs, such as fines for using tolls.

Can I use another person‚Äôs credit card to pay for the rental?

This is no problem at all. But be aware that the secondary individual will automatically be listed as a second driver. Therefore, full documentation and licences must be available before the car pick up is complete.

Can my friend or family member drive the car I rent?

Yes. Most Cruizi car rental providers is offering this service. Often there is an additional charge. Additionally, second driver has to provide a valid license and documentation, there is no reason why they cannot enjoy driving the vehicle.

Can the car rental period be extended?

Of course. It is simple to extend the rental period of any car on our platform. Just get in touch with Cruizi team who will ensure the car is available. After contact, a representative will be in touch to agree a rental extension and arrange payment.

Is there a mileage limit? Do you charge it I go over the limit?

All the rental cars we list on Cruizi come with a standard mileage limit. The number of miles allocated usually depends on the length of the rental agreement. But since we aggregate listings from all of the top car rental agencies in Europe, the mileage limit can vary. A few providers may offer unlimited mileage too. And it is also possible to quote for a bespoke mileage package. In most cases, exceeding the mileage quoted will incur additional costs. Having a good idea of the number of miles needed is a great way to stay in budget.
